What is Industrial Design?

Industrial designs and models are very useful for designers and artists for they allow the protection of the esthetic or ornamental aspect of your objects. It can be three-dimensional- it is then the form or the surface of the object- or two-dimensional, eg, a collection of lines or colors.

IPPRO

IPPRO helps it costumers to make sure of:
- National registration of their Industrial designs and models
- The renewal of their Industrial designs and models
- International registration of their Industrial designs and models
- The search for grandfathering
The protection of the Industrial designs and models is a direct solution for innovations which are not dominant by the technological aspect but rather by the artistic one. Different examples can be used in this regard such as the form of packing such as bottles, or the form of a profile which is used in the domain of aluminum industry The Hague system is an international system administered by the World Intellectual Property Organization which permits the registration of a large number of industrial designs and models through one single application which includes up to 100 industrial design and model belonging to the same class according to Locarno classification for industrial designs and models in about 65 country. The aim of Locarno’s classification is to enable locating industrial designs and models easily by search engines. The protection of an industrial design or model internationally is up to 5 years, renewable twice during that duration.
The registration of your industrial designs and models in Morocco will enable you of getting an accumulated 25 years protection. In fact, the Moroccan law grants a 5 years protection for every industrial design and model renewed 4 times in a row (Law 17/97 – Art. 122) In regards of grandfathering, we help our customers get their industrial designs and models evaluated through specific researches done on the national and international data base of industrial designs and models.

The international database of industrial designs and models, made by the WIPO, enables doing research of grandfathering on the international registrations as well as the data base of national offices.
