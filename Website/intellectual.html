What is Intellectual Property?

Industrial Property Consultancy Firm is a consultancy firm of industrial property known for its long expertise in Industrial Property Portfolio Management and Monitoring of SMEs (Medium-Sized enterprises), universities and Research centers. The key to the firm's success was built on an innovative approach which is based on the following key parameters with a core focus on fostering and enhancing the industrial property of its customer-base:

- Every costumer is unique: It is part of our focus of paying serious attention to what counts to our customers as well as we provide them with advice that are best suited for their needs.
- The customer is our top priority: Our solutions are adapted to suit the individual requirements of each customer in terms of both availability and flexibility.
- Your success means our success: we make all of our resources available to ensure that our customers have the best protection for their industrial property portfolio.

IPPRO

IPPRO takes place at different stages of the creation of the industrial property, including (patent, trademark and registered design), the portfolio monitoring along with the defense of your industrial property rights against any infringements. IPPRO offers professional support throughout the development of the industrial property to provide you with the best consultancy which will enable you to guarantee a better use of your industrial property portfolio.
IPPRO provides a wide partnering network in Europe, USA, Asia, and Africa to ensure the protection of the industrial property portfolio of its customers. The understanding of treaties and international conventions regarding the industrial property is a vital asset to provide our customers with the best avenues of advice.
The choice decision of the mode of protection for a particular industrial property is a crucial step that needs a profound reflexion based on a background knowledge of industrial property’s systems in different countries or regions, and the analysis of the risks that might affect an effective and efficient protection of an industrial property.

IPPRO was founded to provide you with assistance throughout the process of getting an industrial property which involves a multidisciplinary team consisting of experts belonging to different fields related to the right of ownership of an industrial property including:
• Legal consultancy consisting of both the analysis of risks related to the process of creating an industrial property and the best methods to adopt to avoid those risks.
• Technical consultancy to ensure a better provision of your inventions which consist of providing personalized assistance for the preparation of applications for the industrial properties in compliance with the legal requirements in order to benefit from getting the industrial property from the organization responsible for the management of industrial properties in the country in charge.
• The economic consultancy intended to provide a better enhancement of an industrial property which is based on the principal of economic evaluation of the commercial value of the industrial property in regards of a given market.
