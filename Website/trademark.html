What is Trademark?

The trademark which is an identifying mark constitutes the main tool that the majority of companies use. It enables distinguishing the products or services of one enterprise from the products or services of other enterprises to ensure establishing its profile in the market.

Nowadays, the protection of the brand is more than necessary; it is inevitable. IPPRO helps its costumers to make sure of:
- The registration of their brand nationally and internationally
- The renewal of their brand
- Defending their rights is ensured through trademark infringement and the preparation of Opposition proceedings about any trademark, which may harm their brand image.
In regards of prior research, we are equipped with tools to verify the originality of your brand through an advanced research of the databases of national and international brands.
The trademark can come in different forms. The sign of a trademark can be verbal through the use of some names, or it can be figurative as in Logos. Other types of brand also exist such as scent-based and sound-based trade marks… According to the law N°17/97 related to Industrial property in Morocco, the duration for the protection of a trademark is up to 10 years.
Thus, another advantage of a trademark is having an indefinite lifetime, unlike patents and industrial design. It is sufficient to renew the protection every ten years.
